## 4. Core Messaging

- **One-sentence value proposition:** Expense Care helps you track spending, stay within budget, and build savings habits with a fast and simple mobile experience.
- **Top 3-6 features to highlight:**
  1. One-tap expense logging
  2. Daily, weekly, and monthly budget limits
  3. Smart spending summaries with charts
  4. Savings goals and progress tracking
  5. Recurring expense reminders
  6. Multi-currency support
- **What makes Expense Care unique vs alternatives:** It combines strict daily budgeting with goal tracking and an easy-to-use interface designed for habit building, not just expense recording.
